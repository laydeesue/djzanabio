Who is LayDee Sue?

More than just a DJ. LayDee Sue is the new chapter. I bring years of experience behind the decks, now enriched by the depth of motherhood and personal growth.

My sound has evolved into a sophisticated blend of Afro House, Tech House, and Deep Tech. It's not just about mixing tracks; it's about curating an atmosphere where emotion meets energy. From the clubs of Slovakia to international venues, I bring a unique, mature, and vibrant energy to every performance.
